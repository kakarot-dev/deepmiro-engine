01 The Convergence

Multiple streams flowing into a single focal point. Represents thousands of agents converging into one prediction. Echoes the particle animation on the landing page.

02 The Depth Prism

A stylized 'D' built from stacked layers — each layer represents a deeper level of analysis. The negative space between layers suggests the swarm's emergent patterns.

03 The Swarm Lens

An eye/lens formed from scattered particles — the collective swarm "seeing" the future. Merges prediction (lens/focus) with emergence (particles forming a coherent shape).

DeepMiro
04 The Signal

Maximum simplicity. A core signal radiating outward — or a swarm collapsing inward. The ambiguity is the point: convergence and emergence are the same shape. Most scalable, most favicon-friendly.

05 The Neural Shoal

A fish silhouette emerging from a network of connected nodes. Honors the MiroFish heritage while expressing DeepMiro's AI/neural nature. The fish shape is suggested, not drawn — it emerges from the connections, just like predictions emerge from agent interactions.
